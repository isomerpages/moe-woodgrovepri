<h2><strong>English</strong></h2>
<p>At Woodgrove Primary, we believe that every Woodgrovian has the potential
to be developed into effective and confident communicator.</p>
<p>Through our English Programmes, we seek to develop discerning readers,
empathetic communicators and creative inquirers through engaging and purposeful
programmes that promote multi-literacies and appreciation for the language.</p>
<p>In line with the English Language Syllabus 2020, our English Language
department focuses on the development, reinforcement and extension of language
skills through an enjoyment of the language and the promotion of extensive
reading; and leveraging oracy, reading and writing skills to develop knowledge
and independent use of the language.</p>
<p>These are aimed at providing opportunities for Woodgrovians to use English
creatively in a multitude of situations and contexts. We also strive to
develop Woodgrovians into multi-modal literates in this 21st Century.</p>

organises exciting and engaging learning events such as English Language
Fiesta (ELF) and WGPS Book Club. Through activities such as podcasting
and ‘open mike’, Woodgrovians &nbsp;are encouraged to share their views
and spread the joy of learning the English Language with their peers.</p>
